Print a Family Ordinance Request (FOR)

After you've verified and reserved your family names, the final step is to print the Family Ordinance Request (FOR). Then take the FOR to the temple where temple workers print the ordinance cards for you.

 Click to hide all images.Click to show all images.

  1. Click Temple. The temple page displays a list of your reserved ordinances. (See Learn About the Temple Page below for additional information.)
  2. Click the check box next to the name(s) of those for whom you want to do ordinances. (You can select all ordinances by selecting the top check box.)
  3. Click the Print button above the list of ordinances.
  4. The next screen displays a summary of the ordinances you have selected.
    • To select all ordinances, select the top check box next to ORDINANCES.
    • To select only certain ordinances, select the appropriate check boxes.
  5. When you have selected the ordinances you want to include on the Family Ordinance Request, click Print.
  6. On the Print Request screen, click Continue.
  7. Depending on your browser settings, you may see the Family Ordinance request displayed on the screen. Click the Print icon to print it. (If you don’t see it on your screen, you will probably find it in your download folder.)

  8. Close the tab or window showing the Family Ordinance Request (be careful not to close the tab or window showing Family Tree).
  9. When you see a message asking if the Family Ordinance Request was created, click Yes. You’ll be returned to your temple list.
  10. Finally, take the printed Family Ordinance Request to the temple, where temple workers will print the cards you need to perform the ordinances.

Learn About the Temple Page

Click a turquoise rectangle in the illustration below to learn more about that part of the temple page.

These options allow you to view only ordinances in a certain status. Example: to see all ordinances you've shared with the temple or with someone else, click Shared. Note: Printed on these buttons refers to the Family Ordinance Request (FOR), not to cards printed at the temple.

Type text in the filter field to filter the list by word(s). For example, to show only entries containing the name Gunton, type Gunton in the filter field.

Click the check box next to a name, and then click Print to print a Family Ordinance Request (FOR). See Print a Family Ordinance Request (FOR).

Click the check box next to a name, and then click Share to share names with others or with the temple. See Share Names for Temple Work.

Click the check box next to a name, and then click Unreserve to indicate that you want to remove the names from your temple list.

Click the check box next to a name to indicate that you want to take action on that name. The three possible actions are Print, Share, and Unreserve, selected by clicking the appropriate button.

Click the name to see the individual's summary card. Tip: if you want to see the individual's person page without leaving the temple list, hold down Ctrl (Windows) or Command (Mac) while clicking the name.

These icons represent each reserved ordinance:

  • B = Baptism
  • C = Confirmation
  • I = Initiatory
  • E = Endowment
  • SP = Sealing to Parents
  • SS = Sealing to Spouse

To see what the colors mean, click Legend.

This column shows the associated names for any sealing ordinances.

Click Opportunities to see the names of those who may need ordinances in your lines back to your great-great-grandparents. Be sure to verify these names and resolve any duplicates before reserving them.

Click the arrow next to a column heading to see sorting options for the column. Tip: To sort by multiple columns, hold down the Shift key as you select each option.
