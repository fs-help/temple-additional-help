To share a reserved name with family or friends, follow the steps below.

  1. Check the box next to the reserved name you would like to share with someone else.
  2. Click Share, and then click Share with Family or Friends.
  3. Enter the name and email address of the family member or friend you want to share with, and then click Send. Shared reservations will be marked with an envelope icon and will remain on your reservation list until they are accepted by your family member or friend.
  4. When accepted, the reservation will disappear from your list. However, if your invitation is not accepted in two weeks, the share envelope icon will be removed, allowing you to print, share, or unreserve your ancestor reservation again.
