There are many wonderful reasons for doing family history: it strengthens families, provides spiritual protection, increases testimonies, and brings a sense of joy and purpose to those who do it.

But the most important reason may be to help our loved ones beyond the veil receive the ordinances of salvation.

This additional help explains how to prepare and reserve temple names. Here's an overview of the process:

  1. Prepare names in Family Tree to make sure they are ready for temple work.
  2. Resolve any duplicates.
  3. Reserve names for temple work.
  4. Print a Family Ordinance Request and take it to the temple.

You can also share names with family and friends.
